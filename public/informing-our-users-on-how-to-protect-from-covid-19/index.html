Informing our users on how to protect from Covid 19

As the Coronavirus spreads accross the world to a full scale pandemic, we wanted to take our small part in avoiding a dramatic human toll. What we thought could be useful is to inform our users on the key things to do to avoid being infected and the virus to spread among the most vulnerable. It is a simple thing we could implement quickly while we figure out other ways to contribute in tackling this tough situation. The infographic comes in the new notification icon of the app, both in French and English.
